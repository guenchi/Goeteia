3D text labels

(web typeset) lays each label out (canvas-measured widths, CJK-aware wrapping), a hidden 2d canvas rasterizes it once, and the GL side draws camera-facing quads — corners are u_center ± the camera's right and up vectors, (web sdf) turns the raster into a signed distance field, and the fragment shader smoothsteps it around 0.5 — so the edges re-sharpen at any distance: the texture stores geometry, not pixels. Needs WebGL 2. See fx-labels.ss.
