Skeletal animation: the Fox

24 joints pose through the anim-machine state machine each frame — states over clips, crossfading transitions, both clocks running under the fade — and upload as one uniform array; gltf-skin-vs blends four weighted bones per vertex. Keys 1 / 2 / 3: anim-goto! Survey / Walk / Run. See fx-fox.ss.
