Basis Universal, decoded in Scheme

The crate's texture is a 17KB .ktx2 (Basis ETC1S, five mips). (gfx ktx) — written from the Khronos bitstream specification, no C++ transcoder — reconstructs the Huffman codebooks and slices in staging memory and repacks blocks for whatever this GPU speaks: ETC1, BC1, or RGBA where neither extension exists. The tab title reports which family won and the transcode time. See fx-ktx.ss.
