Skybox, clouds, and a mirror ball at sea

The cube map is procedural — deep-blue sky, a hazy horizon, a baked sun, and cumulus from four octaves of 3D value noise over the view direction, so the clouds cross the cube's face seams without an edge — computed as bytes in a Scheme loop and handed to gl-cubemap!. The box draws with the camera's translation erased (a w = 0 multiply), so the sky never moves; the sea ruffles its normal with traveling sines and reflects the same cube map, Fresnel-blended over the deep, and the ball floats in the middle of it. Needs WebGL 2. See fx-skybox.ss.
