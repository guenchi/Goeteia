The arena

Click to capture the mouse, WASD to run, SPACE to jump (onto the crates), click to shoot the drifting orbs — clear a wave and a bigger one spawns. Everything the stack grew, in one page: the packaged character over the broadphase grid at a fixed 120Hz, ray-sphere shots, a (web sprite) HUD from the typeset measurer, (web audio) chirps, raw WebGL through the command buffer.
