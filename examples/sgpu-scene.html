sgl, the WebGPU way

The same declarative scene notation, culled and drawn entirely GPU-side: every mesh joins a geometry group, instances live in storage buffers as matrix + color + bounding sphere, a compute kernel culls them against the frustum, and one drawIndexedIndirect per group draws exactly the survivors. The CPU recomposes only matrices whose signals moved. Needs a WebGPU browser. See sgpu-scene.ss.
