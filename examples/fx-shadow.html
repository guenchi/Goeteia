Soft shadow mapping (PCSS)

Two passes: the boxes render into a depth-only fx-target! from the light's orthographic view (m4-ortho); the scene then reprojects each fragment into light space and asks the map in three steps — a blocker search, a penumbra width from the receiver-blocker gap (an area light's similar triangles), and a PCF exactly that wide. Watch the hovering box: its shadow melts with height while the sitting boxes stay crisp at contact. Needs WebGL 2. See fx-shadow.ss.
