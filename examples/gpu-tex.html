A textured box, on WebGPU

The shader is authored once as the s-expression forms every dialect renders: (uniform sampler2D u_tex) becomes a sampler + texture binding pair in WGSL and (texture2D ...) respells as textureSample. The checkerboard is procedural bytes out of staging memory, shipped by one queue.writeTexture. Needs a WebGPU browser. See gpu-tex.ss.
