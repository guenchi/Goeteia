Deferred shading

The scene rasterizes once into a G-buffer — albedo, world normal and world position, three half-float attachments behind one framebuffer (fx-target-mrt!), filled by a fragment shader with (out 0/1/2 ...) forms. Then 24 moving point lights cost a single fullscreen pass: lighting no longer scales with scene complexity. Needs WebGL 2. See fx-deferred.ss.
