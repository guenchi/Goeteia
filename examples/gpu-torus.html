A lit torus, on WebGPU

(gfx gpu) grows up: indexed u16 geometry from (gfx mesh), a depth buffer that came up with the canvas, and the whole per-frame uniform state — mvp and model — as one 128-byte struct written into a uniform buffer and bound as @group(0) @binding(0). WebGPU has no uniform1f; the struct is the interface. Needs a WebGPU browser. See gpu-torus.ss.
