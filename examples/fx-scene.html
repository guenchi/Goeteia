A reactive scene, raw WebGL

The sgl template is to raw GL what sx is to the DOM: geometry builds and uploads once, and each unquoted attribute is a signal-driven hole. Two signals animate the torus and the sphere; the ground is a (texture ...) mesh, the sphere is (metallic)/(roughness) PBR against a (gfx ibl) probe, and a fourth mesh parked past the far plane is frustum-culled for free. No Three.js. See fx-scene.ss.
