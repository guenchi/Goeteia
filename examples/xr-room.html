A room, into the headset

One scene, two pumps: on a desktop it orbits as usual; where WebXR answers, the button appears and (gfx xr) swaps the loop for the session's rAF, takes each eye's projection and view from the XRPose (written straight into staging memory), and draws once per eye into the session's framebuffer. The command buffer and the shader do not change at all. See xr-room.ss.
